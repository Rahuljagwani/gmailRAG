# Email 1: FSA contribution question

**From:** jmartinez@company.com

**Body:**

> Hi, I'm setting up my elections for next year and I thought the max I could put into my Health FSA was $3,200. A coworker mentioned it went up. Can you confirm what the actual limit is for 2026, and whether there's a minimum I have to contribute too?

**has_clear_answer:** `True`

## Drafted answer

Hi there,

You're correct that the limit went up! For 2026, the maximum you can contribute to your Health FSA is $3,300—an increase from the $3,200 limit you mentioned. There is no required minimum contribution, but if you do contribute, the minimum amount is $120.

Best regards,
Benefits Team
