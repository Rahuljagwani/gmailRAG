**has_clear_answer:** `True`

## Drafted answer

Hi there,

Good news—your coverage will carry over automatically. Since you are currently enrolled, your Critical Illness and Hospital Indemnity coverage will automatically continue in 2026 with the new administrator, Securian, unless you decide to make changes. Your coverage has already been automatically assigned to the closest available option with Securian.

While no action is required if you're satisfied with your coverage, we do encourage you to verify your coverage details on BenefitConnect during open enrollment (October 20–31, 2025) and make any additional adjustments if needed. Any changes you make during that window will take effect January 1, 2026.

Best regards,
Benefits Team
