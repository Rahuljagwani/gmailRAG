## Drafted answer

Hi there,

I'm glad you're ready to enroll in the Long-Term Care Insurance Plan. Here are the enrollment steps:

**Enrollment options:**
- Apply online at **genworth.com/chevron** (available anytime)
- Call **Genworth at 1-800-416-3624**
- Visit **hr2.chevron.com/OpenEnrollment** for additional resources

[Source 4] notes that "you must submit an enrollment application" and provides these resources to "apply for this coverage, learn more about the program, and get a rate quote." Once approved, [Source 4] states that "you will receive a Certificate of Coverage from Genworth within 30 days after you are approved for enrollment in the New LTC plan."

**Important note:** The sources don't specify what plan your manager enrolled in or whether the same plan options are still available now. I'd recommend contacting Genworth or the HR Service Center (1-888-825-5247) to confirm your eligibility and review current plan options before enrolling.

Best regards,
Benefits Team
